BUS SERVICES

The School has a fleet of 20 buses

SRC School Transport Facility School buses are run by the school management and routes are designed to minimize transit times for students as much as possible Schedules are timed to ensure that your chile arrives at the School by 8.15 am everyday.

  • At the end of the school day,the staff ensures that all the students board their respective buses safely.
  • Buses are endorsed with the Speed Governor,GPS tracking system and RFID cards are issued to the students travelling by school buses.
  • All the buses are air-conditioned.
